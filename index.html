About DISTECH

DISTECH is a strategic research initiative at Bergen University College focusing on software technologies for distributed systems. The purpose of the DISTECH research programme is to research and develop new software technologies in order to address the challenges in developing modern complex distributed systems.

The initiative is anchored in the research and development activities at the Department of Computer Engineering within the Faculty of Engineering. The programme currently consists of the following integrated core research areas:

  • Multi-agent systems and logic.
  • Model-driven software development and verification.
  • GRID systems and scientific computing.

The research within DISTECH ranges from theoretical foundations to the development and application computer tools aimed at addressing the challenges in modern software development for distributed systems.
